Intentional Spending Tracker

The Intentional Spending Tracker is a personal finance web application built using Python and Flask, designed to help users better understand and control their spending by comparing intended spending goals against actual financial behaviour.

How it works

The application is based on an intentional budgeting framework where spending is categorised into:

  • Fixed (50%) – Essential and recurring expenses (e.g., rent, bills).
  • Fun (30%) – Discretionary spending (e.g., entertainment, dining).
  • Future (20%) – Savings, investments, and long-term planning.

Directions

To use the tracker:

  1. Click the button below to enter the Dashboard.
  2. Record your Income sources.
  3. Add your Expenses and assign them to one of the three categories.
  4. View the automatic charts to compare your spending against the 50/30/20 goals.
